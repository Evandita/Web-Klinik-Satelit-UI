About Us

Makara Satellite Clinic, University of Indonesia

#YourTrustedPrimaryHealthCare #HealthYellow

Makara Satellite Clinic is a primary care clinic, dedicated to address health problems, i.e., injuries, illnesses, and chronic ailments. The health care professional team consists of family and general practitioner, general dentists, psychologists, nurses, and other health personnel. We provide comprehensive medical service, such as consultations for disease prevention physical examinations, diagnostic tests, prescriptions, and tailored treatment plans.
